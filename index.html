Protocol 01 / The Problem

$786 billion in deferred maintenance.

The American Society of Civil Engineers gives US infrastructure a C- grade. You see it every day. Here's what your city hopes you won't do about it.

01

You hit a pothole

Average vehicle repair cost: $306. AAA reports Americans spend $3 billion annually on pothole damage. Your city knows. They're counting on you not filing a claim.

02

You call 311

You wait on hold. You describe the location. You hang up. Nothing happens for weeks. You call again. They have no record of your first call. You give up. They're counting on that too.

03

Nothing changes

Individual complaints are easy to ignore. There's no public record, no accountability, no consequences. The same potholes reappear every spring. The cycle repeats.

Protocol 02 / The Procedure

Three reports.
Real consequences.

We turned individual frustration into collective power, automated the paperwork, and made inaction legally expensive.

Step · 01
📸

Report in 10 seconds

Snap a photo. GPS drops your pin. AI identifies issue and severity. Quick mode skips the paperwork. Voice mode works hands-free while driving.

Step · 02
✅

Community verifies

When 3 people report the same issue, it's community-verified. Our witness network asks nearby users to confirm — often within minutes.

Step · 03
📨

Authority notified

At 10 reports, we auto-send a professional demand letter to the responsible department — with GPS evidence, photos, fiscal impact projections, and statutory citation.

Step · 04
⏱️

Clock starts running

A public timer tracks the response. Miss the deadline? We generate a formal legal notice citing state statute. The whole community sees who acts and who doesn't.

Protocol 03 / Accountability

Tools that make inaction expensive.

This isn't just a reporting app. It's an accountability platform.

⚖️

Legal demand letters

Auto-generated formal notices citing your state's specific defective highway statute. Tracks the statutory deadline. Creates documented legal exposure.

🛡️

Insurance claim packager

One tap generates a timestamped evidence bundle: GPS proof, photos, notification history, community verification. Everything your insurance company needs.

💰

Fiscal impact math

“Fix this pothole for $75 today, or face $12,000 in liability next year.” We show authorities the math. Inaction becomes the expensive option.

📊

Infrastructure grades

Every city gets a public A-F grade. Response times, fix rates, severity trends — all transparent. See how your city compares.

🗳️

Election district overlay

See which council districts have the most unresolved issues. Know who represents you. Know their record. Vote accordingly.

🏗️

Contractor accountability

Track which repair contractors do quality work and which ones cut corners. When the same pothole opens three months later, we know who to hold responsible.

🎥

Video testimonials

Fifteen-second stories from real people. “This pothole cost me $800.” Attached to escalation emails. Numbers are ignorable. Faces aren't.

🔮

Predictive analytics

We predict where damage will occur before it happens. Seasonal patterns, decay curves, historical data. Pre-deployment reports save cities millions.
