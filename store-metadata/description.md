## Full Description

Tired of dodging the same pothole for months? FixThisFaster puts the power of community reporting in your hands.

REPORT IN SECONDS
Snap a photo, drop a pin, and submit. Our quick-report mode lets you document potholes, broken streetlights, damaged sidewalks, and 20+ other infrastructure issues in under 10 seconds.

COMMUNITY VERIFIED
When 3 people report the same issue, it becomes community-verified — proving it's real and needs attention.

AUTHORITIES NOTIFIED AUTOMATICALLY
After enough community reports, FixThisFaster automatically sends a professional report to the responsible government authority — city, county, or state — with location data, photos, severity ratings, and community impact numbers.

TRACK RESULTS
See response times, fix rates, and authority leaderboards. Know which cities fix problems fast and which ones don't. Hold your local government accountable with real data.

FEATURES
- 24 report categories: potholes, streetlights, sidewalks, drainage, fallen trees, snow/ice, and more
- GPS auto-detection with draggable map pin for precision
- Photo and video documentation with auto-compression
- Voice commands for hands-free reporting while driving
- Bump detection using your phone's sensors
- Offline mode — reports queue and sync when you reconnect
- Community upvoting and confirmation system
- Real-time push notifications on report status changes
- Public dashboard with metrics and heatmaps
- Anonymous reporting supported
- Covers Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and New Hampshire (expanding nationwide)

YOUR TAX DOLLARS AT WORK
Every report you submit helps build a public record of infrastructure problems. Together, we make our communities safer — faster.
