Section I · The Procedure

Three reports.
Real consequences.

Individual complaints die in queues. Documented evidence, multiplied by neighbors, changes the calculus.

I.

Snap a photo

One screen, one tap. The camera opens, AI auto-fills category and severity, GPS drops your pin.

II.

Community verifies

When three neighbors report the same spot, the record becomes community-verified evidence.

III.

Authority notified

At ten reports, a professional demand letter lands in the responsible department's inbox.

IV.

The clock starts

A public countdown under the relevant statute. Miss the deadline, the liability follows.

A report of record.

Every submission is a timestamped notice of defect. This is what one looks like after the community has spoken.
